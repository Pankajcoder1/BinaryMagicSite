  1. What is STL ?
    STL stands for Standard Template Library, is a powerful library in C++ which is collection of data structure, algorithms.
    Components of STL ?
    Their are four components of stl.
    Container Algorithms Iterator Functors
    Why should choose STL ?
    By using this library, you can save time, gain best time and space complexity, finish work in very little line of code. It is mainly known for its versatility, efficiency, and ease of use.
